This is
Two Rivers Magnet Middle School

It is a school for student in grades 6th, 7th, and 8th. It is run by CREC the Capitol Region Education Councel.

Hi!

At Two Rivers, students will cover the same curriculum as they would in their home districts. What makes us unique is our approach to teaching the curriculum. Because we provide chromebooks for all students, we are able to create a more personalized, "blended" approach to learning.
With the aid of technology, teachers are better able to assign readings, videos, and online activities that are appropriate for each student at his or her level. The leveled lessons are available to the students through their online course folders in Schoology. By assigning differentiated assignments online, the teacher is free to work with students in small groups or individually, as opposed to a "one size fits all" whole class approach.
In addition to leveled texts, blended learning offers students the ability to personalize their learning through choices.
